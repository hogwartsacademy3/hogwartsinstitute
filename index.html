About Hogwarts

Hogwarts Institute of Witchcraft and Wizardry is the finest magical school in the wizarding world. Students are trained in magic, ethics, strategy, and mystical knowledge to become powerful and wise witches and wizards.

Hogwarts Houses

  • Gryffindor – Courage and bravery
  • Slytherin – Ambition and cunning
  • Ravenclaw – Intelligence and wisdom
  • Hufflepuff – Loyalty and hard work
